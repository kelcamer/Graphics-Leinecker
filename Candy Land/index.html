When it is your turn, click on the button to draw a card!
If you land on a licorice hole, you lose a turn!
Click on the horse to see who it belongs to!
Press H to go to the default camera view.
Press G to see a top-down view of the board.
Press V to zoom in, and B to zoom out.
Press Y to see the instructions again!
Press A to show which Player each horse represents.
Press Enter to auto-play!

Sometimes two horses will land on the same spot.
When this happens, simply press A again to see both horses at once!

The first person who makes it to the end wins!
