This game is called Don't unleash Charizards fury!
Right now, there are four charizards threatening innocent pikachus!
It is up to you to catch em all!
Each group of pikachus can protect themselves against Charizard, but not for long!
Collect each of the pikachus to win the game!



Game Controls

Use the arrow keys to move left, right, up, and down, respectively!
Press J to jump over the enemies.
Press B to experiment with unique arrow key camera angles!
Press C to see the world map!


Have fun, and good luck!
